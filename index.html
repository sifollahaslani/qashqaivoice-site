QashqAI Voice

A cultural-technology initiative for endangered languages, digital inclusion, and ethical AI.

QashqAI Voice is dedicated to the preservation, revitalization, and digital future of endangered and underrepresented languages. Founded by Siefollah Aslani, the initiative begins with the Qashqai language as a living cultural foundation while building a broader framework for linguistic dignity, cultural memory, and responsible artificial intelligence.

Why It Matters

Thousands of languages remain digitally invisible. When a language disappears from digital systems, it risks disappearing from education, technology, archives, and intergenerational memory. QashqAI Voice responds to this challenge by building ethical, community-centered, and culturally respectful pathways for endangered languages in the age of AI.

Mission

The mission of QashqAI Voice is to protect endangered languages and cultural knowledge through ethical AI, digital inclusion, and community-centered cultural stewardship. This initiative is not only about technology. It is about ensuring that linguistic diversity remains visible, valued, and protected in the digital future.

Core Focus

  • Endangered language preservation
  • Cultural heritage and oral memory
  • Ethical and responsible AI
  • Digital inclusion and accessibility
  • Community-centered participation
  • Non-extractive cultural data practices

QashqAI Voice was founded as a cultural, ethical, and strategic response to the growing digital erasure of endangered and underrepresented languages.

Vision

QashqAI Voice begins with Qashqai, but it is not limited to Qashqai. Its long-term vision is to help build a globally relevant framework where endangered languages can enter digital systems without losing their cultural dignity, community meaning, or historical integrity.

Foundation

The foundational documents of QashqAI Voice include its ethical framework, founding statement, manifesto, legal protections, and strategic roadmap. These materials define the project’s mission, values, governance direction, and long-term development path.
