QashqAI Voice – Preserving Forgotten Voices

QashqAI Voice is an independent, community-led project founded by Siefollah Aslani in Bremen, Germany. The project uses AI technology to preserve endangered languages, support accessibility for migrants and visually impaired users, and promote digital justice.

Our mission is to give forgotten voices global visibility and protect cultural heritage through open and ethical technology.
